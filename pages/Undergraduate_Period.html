Undergraduate Period

This period of my life took place from September 2019 to August 2025, where I studied mechanical engineering at the University of Manitoba. This was a very important period since not only was I able to learn many aspects of mechanical engineering through my classes, but I was able to apply this knowledge to many extracurricular projects. This included me studying rover wheels at a space research laboratory on campus, and leading a subsystem for a rocketry and robotics team. These projects are my proudest aspect of this period, and have their own dedicated page.
